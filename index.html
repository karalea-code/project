PTSD or post-tramatic stress disorder can develop after exposure to a potentially traumatic event that is beyond a typical stressor. Meaning events that are beyond the stress of normal situations.
These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Violent Assult

  • Sexual Assult

  • Natural or Human Caused Disasters
  • Accidents

  • Combat or War

  • Witnessing Acts of Violence
