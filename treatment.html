There are several ways that are known to treat PTSD. New treatments are always being looked into. this is a list of currently accepted treatments and ones in development:

    Medications
  • Exposure therapy is a way to take on stimuli that might cause trauma under the care of therapist. The person becomes desensitized to the stimuli.
  • Cannabis and synthetic cannabinoids research that is currently being done is finding links between useage and decrease of symptoms
  • Acupuncture this study is still "underdevelopment". It will be interesting to see it's use in further development.
  • Movement/Physical Activity while this theory is not perfect, it is a more natural approach to treatment then medacine.
